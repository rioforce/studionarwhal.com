Mary knows she's going to birth an extrordinary Child, but didn't know getting there would be so difficult.

Based on the story of the birth of Jesus Christ, found in The Gospel of [Luke chapters 1 & 2](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Luke%202&version=NKJV) with cross references found in the gospel of [Matthew chapter 1](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Matthew%201&version=NKJV).

---
This is the first of my series of Bible stop-motion animations! I am telling the story of the life and death and resurrection of Jesus Christ with stop-motion.
